About The Company

Delivering Excellence Across Continents Since 2016

Founded in 2016, Stringent Import & Export Ltd. has quickly become a trusted name in international trade and distribution. The vision of Esapa Njang, a New York attorney specializing in international business transactions, Stringent serves as a gateway for premium goods to flow seamlessly between the United States and Africa. With headquarters in Douala, Cameroon, and Bowie, Maryland, we provide exclusive, high-quality products crafted to meet regional preferences and market demands.
